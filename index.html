Click the boxes to test JavaScript Operators on the number below

+=


Adds value on the right side of the equals to the variable on the left

-=


Subtracts the value on the right side of the equals sign from the variable on the left

*=


Multiplies the variable on the left side of the equals sign and the value on the right side of the equals sign

/=


Devides the value in the variable by the value on the right side of the equals sign

++


Placed directly after a variable name, ++ adds one to the variable

--


Placed directly after a variable name, -- subtracts one from the variable
